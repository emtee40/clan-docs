What is Clan?

Clan is a configuration management, secret provisioning, and backup automation framework.

Uniform interface: A single source of truth to manage everything.

Define every component with Nix or JSON files in a single Git repository. From disk layouts and service configurations to access control and backup strategies, scale your infrastructure without relying on centralized providers. With Clan, your machines are under your control.

Automated secret management: Security done right.

Clan keeps your passwords and other sensitive data encrypted, and automatically decrypts them during deployments. By storing them alongside your configurations, your secrets stay safe and easy to manage.

Automated service setup: Deploy services with ease.

Setting up services is a chore: databases must be provisioned, webserver configurations adjusted, and encryption keys generated and shared. Clan's module system makes service setup a breeze.

Automated backups: Keeping everything in sync.

Every Clan service keeps its state in a separate directory, enabling automatic backups of every service – even databases. Use the Syncthing module to create perfect replicas anywhere.

Peer-to-peer mesh VPN: Connect securely and privately.

With Clan, all machines are interconnected by a P2P mesh VPN, keeping your traffic off the public net. Your services, on your hosts, your way.

Live overwrites: Frustration-free remote deployments.

Skip time-consuming setups and manual bootstrapping. Migrate to Clan from any existing Linux system, live over SSH. Deploy configs swiftly and efficiently, maximizing uptime and minimizing hassle.
